# FAQ

## General

### What is EonSwap?

EonSwap is a decentralized exchange (DEX) on Base that allows you to swap tokens, provide liquidity, and farm rewards — all non-custodially.

### Is EonSwap custodial?

No. EonSwap is fully non-custodial. Your funds remain in your wallet at all times. We never have access to your private keys or tokens.

### What network does EonSwap support?

EonSwap is currently deployed on **Base Mainnet** (Chain ID: 8453).

### What wallets are supported?

Any EVM-compatible wallet including MetaMask, Coinbase Wallet, Rainbow, and WalletConnect-compatible wallets.

## Trading

### What are the swap fees?

0.3% per swap, which goes entirely to liquidity providers.

### What is slippage?

Slippage is the difference between expected and actual price. You can set your slippage tolerance in swap settings. Default is 0.5%.

### What is price impact?

Price impact shows how much your trade will move the market price. Large trades on low-liquidity pairs have higher price impact.

### Why did my transaction fail?

Common reasons:
- Insufficient gas (ETH on Base)
- Slippage exceeded (price moved too much)
- Token approval not completed
- Network congestion

## Liquidity

### How do I earn from providing liquidity?

When you add liquidity, you receive LP tokens. You earn 0.3% of all trades in that pair, proportional to your share of the pool.

### What is impermanent loss?

When token prices in a pair diverge significantly, you may end up with less value than if you had just held the tokens. This is called impermanent loss.

### Can I withdraw my liquidity anytime?

Yes. You can remove liquidity at any time by burning your LP tokens.

## Farming

### What rewards can I earn?

You can earn ESTF (governance token) and ESR (bonus reward token) by staking LP tokens in eligible pools.

### How often can I claim rewards?

You can claim rewards at any time. There's no lock-up period.

### What is the emission rate?

Approximately 3.17 ESTF per second, distributed across all farming pools.
