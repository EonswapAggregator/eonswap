## Detailed Breakdown

### 🌾 Farm Rewards (40%)

- **Emission Rate**: ~3.17 ESTF per second
- **Distribution**: Proportional to staked LP tokens
- **Pools**: ESTF/WETH, ESR/WETH, and future pairs

**Yearly Distribution:**
- Year 1: ~100M ESTF
- Year 2: ~100M ESTF
