## What is EonSwap?

EonSwap is a decentralized exchange built on **Base** for swapping tokens, adding liquidity, farming LP positions, and tracking on-chain activity from a single interface.

- **Non-custodial**: You connect your wallet and sign transactions directly.
- **Built for Base**: Fast confirmations and lower gas costs than mainnet Ethereum.
- **Transparent**: Contracts, activity, and leaderboard data are surfaced from on-chain sources.
- **Ecosystem-driven**: ESTF powers incentives today, with farming and referral systems already live.
