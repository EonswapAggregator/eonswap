## What is EonSwap?

EonSwap is a fully decentralized exchange (DEX) built on **Base** — Coinbase's Layer 2 network. We offer:

- **Non-custodial trading** — Your keys, your crypto
- **Low fees** — Base's L2 means gas costs are minimal
- **Transparent** — All contracts are verified on BaseScan
- **Community-first** — 50% of tokens allocated to users (farming + airdrop)
