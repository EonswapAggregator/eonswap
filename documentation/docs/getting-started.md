# Getting Started

Welcome to EonSwap. This guide covers the fastest way to start using the app on Base.

## Prerequisites

- **Wallet**: MetaMask, Coinbase Wallet, or any WalletConnect-compatible wallet
- **Network**: Base Mainnet added to your wallet
- **Gas**: A small amount of ETH on Base for transaction fees
- **Tokens**: ETH or supported ERC-20 tokens for swapping and liquidity

## Your First Swap

1. Visit [EonSwap](https://eonswap.us) and click **Connect Wallet**
2. Select your wallet and approve the connection
3. Open the **Swap** page
4. Choose the token you want to sell, such as ETH
5. Choose the token you want to buy, such as ESTF
6. Enter the amount
7. Review the quote, route, and price impact
8. Click **Swap** and confirm the transaction in your wallet

## Adding Liquidity

1. Open the **Liquidity** page
2. Click **Add Liquidity**
3. Select a token pair, such as `ESTF/WETH`
4. Enter both token amounts
5. Approve tokens if needed
6. Confirm the transaction
7. Receive LP tokens representing your pool share

## Start Farming

1. Open the **Farm** page
2. Find a pool where you already hold LP tokens
3. Click **Stake**
4. Enter the amount and confirm
5. Track rewards as they accrue
6. Harvest or manage the position anytime

## Check Airdrop Status

1. Open the **Airdrop** page
2. Connect your wallet
3. Review the current campaign status
4. If a campaign is active, check eligibility and claim when available
